About FlightSight

FlightSight helps travelers make smarter flight decisions by comparing price, safety, environmental impact, and demand insights — all in one place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make flight booking more transparent and data-driven. Instead of choosing flights based on price alone, we empower users with deeper insights so they can travel smarter and more responsibly.

Who We Are

We are the FlightSight Team, a group of five full-stack developers passionate about building intuitive and impactful travel tools.
